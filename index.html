The Lighthouse Initiative

Uplifting underrepresented minorities to professional success through longitudinal sponsorship.

What is a sponsor?

Advocacy

Sponsors champion their protégé’s visibility. They give protégés their active network connections and make new connections for them.

Spotlight

Sponsors act as spotlights, highlighting their sponsees for opportunities or recognition.

Investment

Sponsors have personal investment in the success of their proteges. Sponsors are personally vested in the upward movement and professional development of their protégé.

IMPORTANT FACTS

85% & 81%

85% women and 81% multicultural professionals need sponsorship to succeed but receive it less than Caucasian men.

65%+

Sponsored minorities are 65% more likely than their unsponsored colleagues to be satisfied with their career advancement.

85% vs 58%

85% vs 58% mothers with and without sponsors, respectively, stay employed.

46% vs 14%

46% men vs 14% women on average, across large companies, have in house "champions".
